APPLE   CATCH

INSTRUCTIONS  TO  PLAY

This is a simple game to have fun in your free time!
Just a few instruction before you get started :
 Click on the green flag at the top left corner followed by the "START" button to start the game.
 Click on the red button at the top left corner to stop and restart the game at any point.
 You need to score 30 points to win this game.
 There are different apples which earn different number of points :
 Red apple : score + 1
 Golden apple : score + 2
 Raw green apple : score - 5
 Move the basket using the LEFT ARROW and RIGHT ARROW keys to catch your apples.

So that is it! HAVE FUN!
